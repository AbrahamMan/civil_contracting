Worker

Worker record is a Master for Workers linked with Employee Master.

Civil Contracting > Documents > Worker > New

Fill in the details of Wage Slip.

  • Employee: Link to Employee if Employee Master is already created, not mandatory
  • Project: Project on which worker is working
  • Full Name: Full Name of Worker (type in if Employee is not Selected)
  • Workstation: Workstation on which the worker is working e.g Mason Civil, Coolie Helper, etc
  • Outstanding Wages: Automatically accumulated outstanding wages as per records in Worker Sheet, do not unnecessarily modify
  • Hour Rate: Hour rate of the worker
  • Contact No.: Contact Phone number
